# Team & Workload

The Team page helps you see who's on your team, what they're working on, and whether anyone is overloaded or has capacity.

---

## 1. Accessing the Team Page

Click **Team** in the sidebar, or navigate to `/dashboard/team`.

---

## 2. Team Member Cards

The page displays a grid of **member cards**, one per team member. Each card shows:

- **Name and avatar** — the person's identity
- **Role** — their organization role (Owner, Admin, or Member)
- **Job title** — if they set one (e.g., "Frontend Developer")
- **Department** — if they set one (e.g., "Engineering")
- **Task count** — number of active tasks assigned to them
- **Workload bar** — a visual indicator of their workload

### Workload Bar

The workload bar is color-coded:

| Color | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Green** | Normal workload — below or at average task count |
| **Yellow** | High workload — above average, getting busy |
| **Red** | Overloaded — significantly above average, may need help |

The average is calculated across all team members in the organization.

---

## 3. Inviting New Team Members

### From the Team Page

1. Click **Invite Member** on the Team page.
2. Fill in:
   - **Email** (required) — the person's email address
   - **Role** — Admin or Member (Owner is reserved for the org creator)
   - **Job Title** (optional)
   - **Department** (optional)
3. Click **Send Invitation**.

### What Happens

- An **invitation email** is sent with a link to join
- The invitation is valid for **7 days**
- When they accept:
  - They become an organization member
  - A PA profile is automatically created for them
  - They can be added to projects

### Using Your PA

> "Invite alex@company.com to the team as a member"

### Who Can Invite

Only **Owners** and **Admins** can invite new members.

---

## 4. Member Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|------------|
| **Owner** | Full control: manage org, invite anyone, delete projects, manage all settings. Only one per org (the creator). |
| **Admin** | Manage org settings, invite members, create and manage all projects. |
| **Member** | Create projects, manage tasks in their projects, post messages, use the PA. |

### Changing a Member's Role

1. On the Team page, click the options menu on a member's card.
2. Select **Change Role**.
3. Choose the new role.
4. Click **Save**.

Only Owners and Admins can change roles. You can't change the Owner role.

---

## 5. Removing a Team Member

1. On the Team page, click the options menu on a member's card.
2. Select **Remove from Organization**.
3. Confirm the removal.

When removed:
- They lose access to the organization and all projects
- Their tasks become unassigned (not deleted)
- Their messages and comments remain (attributed to their name)
- They can be re-invited later

---

## 6. Checking Workload via PA

Your PA can analyze team workload with more detail than the visual bars:

> "Who's overloaded on the team?"
> "Does anyone have capacity for a new task?"
> "What's Sarah working on?"
> "How many tasks does each person have?"

The PA gives a narrative response with specific numbers and recommendations.

---

## 7. Tips

1. **Check the Team page before assigning tasks** — see who has capacity
2. **Ask the PA for workload reports** — "Who's overloaded?" gives more context than the visual bars
3. **Keep job titles and departments updated** — this helps the PA tailor reports and suggestions
4. **Review the yellow/red bars** — if someone is consistently red, discuss redistribution
