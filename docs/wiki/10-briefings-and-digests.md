# Morning Briefing & Digests

Hive's PA proactively keeps you informed without you having to ask. This page covers your morning briefing, weekly digest, and automatic nudges.

---

## 1. Morning Briefing

Every morning, your PA prepares a personalized briefing about your day ahead.

### What's In the Briefing

- **Tasks due today** — what you need to finish today
- **Tasks due this week** — what's coming up
- **Overdue tasks** — anything past its deadline
- **Today's meetings** — from your connected calendar (Google or Microsoft)
- **Blockers** — any blocked tasks on your projects
- **Recent activity** — what happened in your projects since your last briefing
- **Suggested focus** — the PA recommends what to prioritize

### Example Briefing

> Good morning, Sarah. You have 3 tasks due today, including the high-priority "Review API design" for Website Redesign. You've got two meetings — a standup at 9:30 and a design review at 2pm. One thing to watch: the "Database migration" task has been blocked for 2 days waiting on DevOps credentials. I'd suggest tackling the API review first since Alex is waiting on your feedback. Your afternoon is free after the design review — good time for deep work.

### Where Briefings Appear

1. **Dashboard Home page** — a briefing card at the top of your dashboard
2. **Notification** — delivered via your preferred channel (in-app, email, or Slack)
3. **PA chat** — you can always ask "What's my briefing?"

### Configuring Your Briefing

1. Go to **Settings > PA Settings**.
2. Under **Briefings**:
   - **Morning briefing** — toggle on/off
   - **Time** — set when you want it (default: 8:45 AM in your timezone)
3. The briefing is generated at your specified time (within a 15-minute window).

### Manually Requesting a Briefing

You don't have to wait for the scheduled time. Ask your PA anytime:

> "What should I focus on today?"
> "Give me my morning briefing"
> "What's on my plate?"

## 2. Weekly Digest

At the end of each week, your PA summarizes the week's accomplishments and previews the week ahead.

### What's In the Digest

- **Task completion rate** — how many tasks were completed vs. total
- **Tasks completed** — what you (and your team) shipped
- **Tasks carried over** — what didn't get done and rolled to next week
- **New blockers** — any blockers that appeared this week
- **Project milestones** — completed milestones or significant progress
- **Team highlights** — who shipped what, standout contributions
- **Next week preview** — upcoming deadlines and meetings

### Configuring Your Weekly Digest

1. Go to **Settings > PA Settings**.
2. Under **Briefings**:
   - **Weekly digest** — toggle on/off
   - **Day** — choose which day (default: Friday)
3. The digest is delivered at the end of your working hours on the chosen day.

### Delivery Channels

The digest is sent via your preferred notification channel:
- **In-app** — appears as a notification and in the PA chat
- **Email** — full digest sent to your email
- **Slack** — sent as a Slack DM

---

## 3. End-of-Day Digest (Optional)

An optional shorter digest at the end of your workday.

1. Go to **Settings > PA Settings**.
2. Under **Briefings**:
   - **End-of-day digest** — toggle on/off (default: off)
   - **Time** — set when (default: 5:30 PM)

This is lighter than the morning briefing — it summarizes what you accomplished today and flags anything left open.

## 4. Proactive Nudges

Your PA also sends automatic nudges to help you stay on track. These aren't briefings — they're targeted reminders.

### Overdue Task Nudge

- **When:** Hourly check
- **Trigger:** You have a task past its due date that isn't completed or cancelled
- **Frequency:** At most once per 24 hours per task (no spam)
- **Example notification:**

> "The task 'Review design mockups' was due yesterday. Want me to update the deadline or mark it done?"

### Stale Task Detection

- **When:** Daily at 10:00 AM UTC
- **Trigger:** You have a task marked "In Progress" that hasn't been updated in 7+ days
- **Example notification:**

> "The task 'Implement user auth' hasn't been updated in 9 days. Is it still in progress?"

### How to Respond to Nudges

You can respond naturally:

> "Yes, extend the deadline to next Friday"
> "Mark it as done"
> "It's still in progress, I'm working on it"
> "Cancel that task"

---

## 5. Managing Notification Volume

If you feel like you're getting too many nudges:

1. **Adjust working hours** — nudges only fire during your working hours (set in PA settings)
2. **Change notification channel** — switch from in-app to email for less interruption
3. **Disable specific briefings** — toggle off any briefing type you don't need
4. **Complete or close stale tasks** — the nudges stop once tasks are resolved
