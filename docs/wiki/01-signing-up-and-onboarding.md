# Signing Up & Onboarding

This guide walks you through creating your Hive account, setting up your first organization, and completing your profile.

---

## 1. Create Your Account

1. Go to your Hive instance URL (e.g., `https://your-hive-app.com`).
2. Click **Sign Up** on the landing page.
3. You can sign up with:
   - **Email and password** — enter your email, choose a password, and verify via the confirmation email.
   - **Google account** — click "Continue with Google" for one-click signup.
   - **Microsoft account** — click "Continue with Microsoft."
4. Once verified, you'll be redirected to the **onboarding flow**.

---

## 2. Create or Join an Organization

After sign-up, Hive needs to know which workspace you belong to.

### Creating a New Organization

1. On the onboarding page, select **Create a new organization**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Organization name** — your company or team name (e.g., "Acme Corp").
   - **Slug** — a URL-friendly identifier auto-generated from the name (e.g., `acme-corp`). You can customize it.
3. Click **Create Organization**.
4. You are automatically assigned the **Owner** role with full permissions.
5. A default PA (Personal Assistant) profile is created for you in this organization.

### Joining an Existing Organization

If someone on your team already created an organization:

1. Check your email for an **invitation link** from Hive.
2. Click the link — it takes you to sign up (if you don't have an account) or directly to the organization.
3. Once you accept, you become a **Member** of that organization.
4. A PA profile is automatically created for you.

---

## 3. Complete Your Profile

After creating or joining an organization, you'll see the profile setup screen.

1. **Job Title** (optional) — e.g., "Frontend Developer", "Product Manager." This helps your PA and team understand your role.
2. **Department** (optional) — e.g., "Engineering", "Design."
3. Click **Continue to Dashboard**.

You can always update your profile later from **Settings > Profile**.

---

## 4. What Happens Behind the Scenes

When you complete onboarding, Hive automatically:

- Creates your **PA profile** with sensible defaults:
  - Autonomy mode: **Copilot** (the PA asks before executing sensitive actions)
  - Communication style: **Concise** and **Professional**
  - Morning briefing: **Enabled** at 8:45 AM in your timezone
  - Weekly digest: **Enabled** on Fridays
- Sets your timezone based on your browser
- Sets default working hours (9:00 AM – 5:00 PM)

All of these can be customized in [PA Profile & Preferences](./17-pa-profile-and-preferences.md).
